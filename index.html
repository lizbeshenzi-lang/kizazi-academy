Our Philosophy

Education Rooted in Heritage, Reaching for the World

At Kizazi — meaning "generation" in Swahili — we believe the most powerful education honours where a child comes from while preparing them for where they are going.

Our Karen campus is a sanctuary of intentional learning: small classes, world-class facilities, and educators who understand that a child's confidence is built one discovery at a time.
